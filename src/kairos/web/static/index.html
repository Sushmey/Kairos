Admin · how Kairos decides

Kairos is a contextual bandit: it learns when to interrupt you with bookmark clusters. Every s it reads your headspace, ranks clusters, runs interrupt gates, and either stays silent or surfaces one digest. In demo mode, click Surface now when you're ready — no auto-cycle on load. The log on the left is the live trace; panels on the right summarize the latest outcome.
